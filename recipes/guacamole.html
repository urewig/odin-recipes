Gaucamole

This classic guacamole with creamy avocados, fresh cilantro, jalapeno, and red onion is ready to serve in just minutes.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoons red onion, chopped
  • ⅔ bunch cilantro, stems cut off and leaves chopped
  • ⅔ jalapeno, minced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 medium (blank)s roma tomatoes, seeded and diced
  • 3 medium (blank)s Avocados from Mexico, cubed

Steps

  1. Mix diced onion, cilantro, jalapeno, lime juice and garlic.
  2. Add tomatoes and mix.
  3. Gently fold in avocados.
  4. Serve with fresh veggies or tortilla chips.

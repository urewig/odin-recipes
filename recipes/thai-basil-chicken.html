Thai Basil Chicken

This only takes about 25 minutes to make.
Very easy and fabulous flavor! This is very popular in Thailand.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • ½ onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1 large skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • ¼ cup oyster sauce
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 pinch white sugar
  • 2 small chile peppers, sliced
  • ¼ cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoons water
  • ½ cup fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  3. Add chicken; cook and stir until no longer pink, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in oyster sauce, soy sauce, and sugar. Stir in chile peppers.
  5. Pour in water. Cook until slightly thickened, 3 to 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in basil before serving.
